Resources | Data

ArcGIS Living Atlas - Indicators of the Planet
See the latest in global issues with links to their respective open data portals.
ASTER Volcano Archive
The world’s largest specialty archive of volcano data, including publicly available high-resolution multispectral ASTER data.
Climate Data Online Search
Find past weather and climate data provided by NOAA’s National Centers for Environmental Information (NCDC).
Data.gov
The home of the U.S. Government’s open data.
Earth Engine Data Catalog
Public data catalog that includes a variety of standard Earth science raster datasets.
EarthData
Access thousands of unique data products in NASA’s Earth Observing System Data and Information System (EOSDIS) collection from a variety of sources including the International Space Station, satellites, airborne campaigns, field campaigns, in-situ instruments, and model outputs.
EarthExplorer
An online search, discovery, and ordering tool developed by the United States Geological Survey (USGS) that supports the searching of satellite, aircraft, and other remote sensing inventories through interactive and textual-based queries.
EarthWorks
Search, download, and explore geospatial data shared across a multi-academic consortium.
Elfelt Server List
(PDF) list of 50+ federal, 150+ state, 200+ county, and 200+ city GIS servers in the United States.
European Commission Joint Research Centre - ESDAC
European landslide inventory.
FAO GeoNetwork
Provides interactive maps, satellite imagery and related spatial databases for sustainable development and decision making in agriculture, forestry, fisheries and food security.
FEC Elections and Voting
Every two years, the Federal Election Commission publishes Federal Elections, a compilation of official, certified federal election results; these include primary, runoff, and general election results for the Senate, the House of Representatives and (when applicable) the President.
Fuzzy Gazeteer
Name search for feature locations from all over the world.
GeoPlatform
Using collaboration, common data, services, applications and shared infrastructure to address national and regional issues and priorities (The Federal Geographic Data Committee).
GeoQuery
Enables individuals and organizations of all skill levels to freely find and aggregate satellite, economic, health, conflict, and other spatial data into a single, simple-to-use file compatible with Microsoft Excel and other common software (Goodman et al., 2019).
Google Dataset Search
Find datasets stored hosted in thousands of repositories across the Web through simple keyword search.
Harmonized World Soil Database v 1.2
Global elevation, slope, and aspect data in 5 min and 30 arc second resolutions.
Humanitarian Data Exchange (HDX)
Find, share and use humanitarian data all in one place.
ISRIC SOTER Database
Soil and terrain maps for South America, Africa (parts), Europe (parts), and Asia (parts) from 1986 to 2016.
Kaggle
Explore, analyze, and share quality data in an accessible, non-proprietary format where each dataset is a community where you can discuss data, discover public code and techniques, and create your own projects.
National Land Cover Database (NLDC)
Provides spatial reference and descriptive data for characteristics of the land surface such as thematic class (for example, urban, agriculture, and forest), percent impervious surface, and percent tree canopy cover.
Natural Earth
A public domain map dataset featuring vector and raster data.
OpenTopography
OpenTopography aims to democratize access to high-resolution topographic data in a manner that serves users with varied expertise, application domains, and computing resources.
Global Shark Attack File
A time-stamped semi-geolocated incident log of global shark attacks.
SRTM 30-m DEM
Derek Watkins’s interactive map for easily downloading Shuttle Radar Topography Mission (SRTM) thirty meter (30 m) resolution elevation tiles from NASA servers.
TanDEM-X Science Server
Global 12, 30, and 90 m digital elevation models.
Thematic Mapping World Borders
Esri shapefile of the world’s country borders in two resolutions. CC BY-SA 3.0 by Bjørn Sandvik 2009.
TopoView
A digital repository of USGS 1:250,000 scale and larger (more detailed) maps printed between 1884 (the inception of the topographic mapping program) and 2006.
US Census Bureau
The leading source of quality data about the US’s people and economy.
USDA Web Soil Survey
Soil data for the US and its territories.
USGS Continents
Esri shapefile for the major continent boundaries provided from the Archival Policies and Collections Database for the Woods Hole Science Center’s Marine Sediment Samples: U.S. Geological Survey Open-File Report 2006-1187, version 1.0 (Buczkowski & Kelsey, 2007).
USGS The National Map
GIS data download and visualization services provided by the U.S. Geological Survey.
Virginia GIS Clearinghouse
A repository of geospatial data produced and used by state agencies in Virginia (see Virginia Geospatial Clearinghouse Nodes for open data portals of every available county or municipality in VA).
